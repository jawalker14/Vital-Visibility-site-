About Us

We’re transforming healthcare marketing in South Africa.

Our Mission

We help South African healthcare professionals grow online — ethically, compliantly, and with measurable results. Our AI‑powered approach blends strategy, design, and performance marketing to earn patient trust and practice growth.

Our Story

Founded by Jack Walker, Vital Visibility began with a simple insight: many private practices in South Africa struggle to navigate the digital landscape while remaining compliant with HPCSA and POPIA regulations. Drawing on years of experience in healthcare marketing and a passion for ethical AI technology, Jack created Vital Visibility to bridge this gap. Today, we help clinics, dentists, specialists and allied health professionals across the country build a trustworthy digital presence that attracts and retains patients.

Our Values

Ethics

We adhere to HPCSA guidelines and build compliant marketing solutions that respect patient trust.

Clarity

We communicate transparently, simplify the complex, and deliver clear reporting on performance.

Speed

Results

Your growth is our goal. We focus on measurable outcomes, from bookings to brand awareness.
